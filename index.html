    How many dice to play with?

    Rules

    Minimum players 2

    Single die

    • The goal of the game is to accumulate 100 points (or more)
    • Player can roll the dice as long as they want, unless
    1. player throws 1.
    2. decides to hold.

    Two dice

    • If both of the dice are the same, their sum is doubled
    • If you throw double 1's you'll earn 25 points
    • If you throw a single 1, your turn ends
